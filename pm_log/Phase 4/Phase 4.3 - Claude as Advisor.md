# Phase 4.3 — Claude as Advisor

**Goal:** After months of working together, Claude has a perspective on how you work — not just patterns, but observations, blind spots, and honest assessments that change how you operate. It surfaces these at the right cadence, without being asked.

**Status:** Not Started
**Depends on:** Phase 3.3 complete (Pattern Memory must exist) + real data corpus (6+ months of usage strongly preferred)

---

## The Distinction From Pattern Memory

Phase 3.3 is reactive: "You're about to do X — quick note, tasks like this usually take you 40% longer." It surfaces signals in the moment.

Phase 4.3 is proactive: "Looking at your last 6 months, there's something worth naming." It's not triggered by a specific action — it's a standing advisor relationship that surfaces insights on a cadence, based on accumulated evidence.

The difference in practice:
- **Pattern Memory (3.3):** "This action may take longer than you think." (contextual, immediate)
- **Claude as Advisor (4.3):** "Your prospecting outcomes have a systemic problem — you always start late in the week and run out of time. This has happened 8 times." (retrospective, strategic)

---

## What This Phase Delivers

By the end of 4.3:
- A weekly retrospective appears in-app every Friday (or Monday), generated by Claude
- The retrospective contains 2-3 substantive observations based on recent outcome and results data
- Claude can proactively flag a strategic concern when the evidence is strong enough
- An Advisor history view lets you look back at past observations

---

## Scope

### Weekly Retrospective

**When:** Friday at 5:30pm (same send as EOD Wrap, but Fridays get an expanded version). Or Monday at 8:00am — PM and user to decide at kickoff based on which feels more useful (Friday = close the week, Monday = orient the week).

**Delivered:** In-app notification (a dot on the sidebar) + expanded content in a new "Weekly Review" panel. Optionally also in the Slack EOD Wrap on Fridays.

**Structure:**
```
Week of Feb 17 — Weekly Review
────────────────────────────────────

What happened:
4 outcomes closed · 11 actions done · 3h 40m in Focus sessions

What's worth noting:
1. You closed 3 of 4 outcomes on time this week. The one that slipped
   (prospecting) was started Wednesday — your pattern is to start these
   mid-week, and they usually need more time than that. 9 of 12 prospecting
   outcomes in your history started mid-week. Starting on Monday may help.

2. Your email campaigns this week got strong open rates but the booking
   conversion is still below target (2.1% vs 5% goal). This is the 4th
   week in a row. The pattern is consistent — it's worth a focused session
   on the CTA strategy.

3. Your Focus sessions averaged 74 minutes this week (your stated ideal is
   90). You may be breaking out early. Worth checking in: are the sessions
   ending naturally or are you getting pulled away?

────────────────────────────────────
No action required. These are observations, not tasks.
```

**Tone:** Claude is a trusted advisor, not a performance manager. It never says "you failed" — it says "here's what the data suggests." It names patterns, not judgments. If nothing meaningful is worth saying, it says "Solid week. Nothing unusual to report." (Not silence — a brief acknowledgment.)

### Proactive Strategic Flags

Beyond the weekly cadence, Claude can surface a strategic observation when:
- A pattern crosses a threshold that makes it undeniable (e.g. same outcome type has missed 5 times in a row)
- A deadline cluster is forming that hasn't been flagged in the weekly review yet
- A behavior change is statistically detectable (e.g. Focus session lengths declining over 3 weeks)

These surface as a subtle in-app flag: a small "Claude has an observation →" link in the sidebar. Clicking it opens the observation. It doesn't interrupt — it waits for you to engage.

**Hard constraints:**
- Maximum one proactive flag per week (not per day, not per session)
- Only surfaces when evidence is strong (same thresholds as Pattern Memory: 8+ data points in category)
- Never in the Slack brief
- Never phrased as an alert or alarm — always framed as a neutral observation

### Claude Prompt for Retrospective Generation

Claude receives:
- All outcomes closed in the last 7 days (titles, results, hit/miss, result notes)
- All outcomes that slipped or were deferred
- Focus session summary (count, average duration, which outcomes)
- Relevant pattern_observations from Phase 3.3 (particularly ones with new data points)
- User's context snapshot

Instruction: "Write a weekly retrospective for a personal execution OS. Structure: 1 sentence summary of what happened this week, then 2-3 numbered observations based on the data. Each observation should name a pattern, give evidence, and suggest a possible implication — but not prescribe action. Tone: trusted advisor, not performance manager. If nothing significant is worth observing, say so in one line. Plain text, no markdown headers or bold."

### Advisor History View

A new panel accessible from the sidebar: "Advisor" or "Weekly Reviews."

Shows:
- A list of past weekly retrospectives (date, first line preview)
- Clicking one opens the full text
- Proactive flags are also logged here

This creates a record of your working history as interpreted by Claude — a kind of executive journal, generated automatically.

---

## Out of Scope

- Claude initiating conversation (it observes and surfaces, you engage on your terms)
- Advisor sending unsolicited Slack DMs outside the established briefing cadence
- Goal-setting or OKR features (the advisor observes, it doesn't set targets)
- Sharing advisor observations externally

---

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Weekly retrospective generates on correct day/time (Friday or Monday — confirm at kickoff)
- [ ] Retrospective contains what-happened summary + 2-3 substantive observations
- [ ] Observations are grounded in real data (no hallucinated patterns)
- [ ] Tone is consistently advisory, not judgmental
- [ ] If nothing notable: Claude says so (no fabricated observations)
- [ ] Proactive flags surface in sidebar when threshold evidence exists
- [ ] Maximum one proactive flag per week enforced
- [ ] Advisor history view shows all past retrospectives and flags
- [ ] All observations respect Pattern Memory floors (8+ data points per category)
- [ ] Engineer + PM sign-off
